Allow your viewers to see the metadata (exif, xml, gps, author, copyright etc.) of the pictures in your posts.

== Description ==

"View metadata on Metapicz" adds a link to the pictures in your posts that allows your viewers to see the metadata (exif, xml, gps, author, copyright etc.) of the pictures in your posts.  The link will open a new tab to the metadata page on metapicz.com ([example picture](http://metapicz.com/#landing?imgsrc=http%3A%2F%2Fregex.info%2Fi%2FJF7_111890.jpg)).

== Installation ==

1. Upload `metapicz-wp.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Done!

== Screenshots ==

1. Each picture in your posts has a link to the metdata page on Metapicz.com
